Those who fall under the category “without twik” are visitors that are not exposed to twik’s personalizations, in some cases due to non-eligibility, and in other cases, these visitors are purposely excluded from experiencing automated personalizations in order for twik to understand what personalizations work for which segments, to generate an increase in sales and revenue.
